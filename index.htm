Blume Ventures provides seed funding in the range of $50K - $250K to early-stage tech-focused/tech-enabled ventures. We are proponents of a collaborative approach and like to co-invest with like-minded angels and seed funds. We then provide follow-on investments to our stellar portfolio companies, ranging between $500K and $1.5mill.
 
We are conscious that capital alone can't drive great companies from seed to venture rounds. A growing network of advisors, mentors and domain experts.... - Blumiers, as we call ourselves - provide active hands-on support to nurture, grow and scale these companies.
